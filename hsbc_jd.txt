About the job
Some Careers Have More Impact Than Others.

If you’re looking for a career where you can make a real impression, join HSBC and discover how valued you’ll be.

HSBC is one of the largest banking and financial services organisations in the world, with operations in 62 countries and territories. We aim to be where the growth is, enabling businesses to thrive and economies to prosper, and, ultimately, helping people to fulfil their hopes and realise their ambitions.

We are currently seeking an experienced professional to join our team in the role of Analyst

Principal Responsibilities

 Work on client strategic reviews for priority clients which includes discussions with different product partners to prepare action points for achieving the client plan 
 Manage quarterly updates to Innovation Banking pitch materials, incorporating sector information, pricing, and design updates in coordination with stakeholders. 
 Support sector-specific content creation for international and US Innovation ecosystems, including sub-sector analysis, cross-border financing trends, and contributions to thematic reports. 
 Work with relationship managers (RM) on creating new sales decks and collateral to support banker-led client meetings, including one-pagers and tailored content for specific sectors 
 Work with relationship managers (RM) on deals by understanding client needs through discussions hosted by RMs, running internal deal profitability tools as per requirements, coordinating with internal teams like credit, risk, on-boarding and markets for deal execution 
 Support RMs by preparing insightful portfolio analysis focused on returns against plan, breakdown by products, regions, sector for senior management intelligence 
 Undertake business development initiatives for identifying growth prospects specific to clients by using public information as well as licensed data providers 
 Analyze broker reviews held periodically with clients for global markets product to understand our strong areas and opportunities as per client strategy 
 Work with RMs for document negotiation with clients like NDA, ISDA, GMRA and others by coordinating with internal teams like legal, treasury and risk 
 Undertake thought leadership projects by working on sector and client relevant themes and preparing pitch-books to help RMs to originate and deliver business opportunities 
 Understand and ensure compliance with all relevant internal and external rules, regulations and procedures that apply to the conduct of the business 
 Undergo continuous learning and enhance job specific skills over-time 
 May be required to travel overseas as per business needs 
 Pro-actively suggest and design improvements in process workflow, where applicable 
 Participate in team meeting / team activities and work towards sustaining team spirit 
 Support peers in managing day-to-day workstreams as required 
 Become subject matter expert in respective client sectors 
 Act as a role model for our Group Values and Behaviors supporting colleagues and customers 
 Support achievement of team objectives & participate in in development of cohesive teams 
 Contribute to the creation of a supportive work environment driven by people centric values 
 Build professional relationships with colleagues in other areas 
 Actively participate and share own ideas in team discussions for overall development 

Qualification

 Exceptionally qualified MBA’s (with good CGPA score) and CA’s with Big four (EY, PWC, Deloitte, KPMG) experience 
 Ability to establish rapport with team members and product partners 
 Excellent quantitative and analytical skills 
 Strong interpersonal skills, coupled with the ability to succeed within a matrix management structure and build and maintain global team relationships 
 Highly proficient at Excel, PowerPoint and Word 
 Good at multitasking 
 Strong attention to detail 
 Highly motivated; strong work ethic; thrives under pressure, confident, team player
